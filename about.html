About Us

Who we are and what drives us.

Who We Are

Groundline Cleaning & Hospitality Services is a Qatar‑based company providing professional cleaning, hospitality, construction and project management solutions. Our team of well‑trained staff delivers services tailored to your schedule, budget and expectations.

We operate through two divisions: the Groundline division, which focuses on cleaning and hospitality, and the Underline Trading & Contracting division, which manages construction and contracting projects. Together, these divisions allow us to serve a broad range of clients with diverse needs.

How We Work

  1. Understanding client needs: We listen carefully to understand your requirements.
  2. Tailored solutions: We design customised service plans based on your schedule, budget and expectations.
  3. Professional deployment: We assign well‑trained manpower such as cleaners, chefs, waiters or stewards for the job.
  4. Quality service delivery: We deliver with attention to detail using safe and effective methods and continuously monitor performance and seek feedback.
  5. Project execution & quality delivery: For construction projects, we emphasise planning, execution and quality delivery.
